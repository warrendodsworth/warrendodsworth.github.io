Insight 91 came from a simple frustration: I could not see market data in the way I wanted to in order to make confident long-term decisions.

## Focus

- simplify market analysis for long-term and passive investors
- combine multiple indicators into a clearer, more teachable view of market conditions
- reduce the gap between raw data and confident action

## Product direction

The product was shaped around clarity and actionable intelligence rather than complexity for its own sake. The goal was not just to show indicators, but to make them easier to interpret through visual cues, explanations, and a more readable framing of market health.

## Why it matters

Insight 91 reflects a broader product pattern in my work: building tools that help people cut through noise, understand what they are looking at, and make better-informed decisions.
