Wiseguy Investor was built around a simple product idea: help people who are curious about investing move from vague interest into concrete first actions.

## Focus

- make a finance-adjacent product feel less intimidating to new users
- connect product direction with practical implementation
- build something that combined explanation, onboarding, and a clearer user journey

## Role

This was a hands-on product and development effort, which meant moving between concept framing, technical execution, and the shape of the user experience rather than treating those as separate tracks.

- the product framing and user problem definition
- the most important technical decisions in the implementation
